Melbourne is the state capital and the most populous city in the state of Victoria. It is the second most populous city in Australia, next to Sydney. Encompassing an area of 3858 square miles, this city extends upto Dandenong and Macedon mountain ranges, Mornington Peninsula and Yarra Valley. As per the 2016 census, the pooulation was 4,725,316, and the inhabitants are called Melburnians.
